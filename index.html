Basketball

If you want to play basketball there's certain equipment you need to have and certain things you need to do to be fully ready to play basketball.

How to Play Basketball:

Basketball is a very mobile sport that requires a lot of running. The game starts off with two even teams (most of the time teams of 5) and one person from each team jump in the center of the court for the ball first. Whoever gets the ball that team gets to try and score at the basketball first. The other team will be playing defense and trying to stop your team from scoring. When you shoot and the team gets the rebound or when you score you go to the other side of the court and the other team will try and score at the opposite basket. Points are counted by twos if you are in front of the 3 point line and they count by threes if you make a shot behind the 3 point line. If a person is fouled that person gets to shoot 2 or 3 free throws depending on where they are on the court. If you are in front of the 3 point line you will get 2 shots and if you are behind the 3 point line you will get 3 shots. However if you make the shot while being fouled they will counted the points and you will get to shoot 1 more free throw no matter where you are. They only difference is if you shoot a 2 pointer it will be a chance for a 3 point play and if you shoot a 3 pointer you will get a chance for a 4 point play. The team the ends up with the most points at the end of 4 quarters wins the game.

Equipment:

When playing basketball there’s some specific equipment that is needed:

Sneakers are a very important thing to have because if a person is playing in dress shoes or sandals there is a very good chance that person will get hurt.

Have a pair of shorts and a T-Shirt when playing because if a person is playing in a sweater and jeans you will get very very warm and you might dehydrate or pass out and that is not safe for the game.

Have a basketball to play with and practice with.

This doesn’t have to be worn but I really recommend this, I recommend that you get knee pads, arm sleeves, a mouth piece, and an ankle brace. Now I don’t think you should get this stuff if you are absolutely healthy but if you are feeling pain in any part of you body while playing basketball you should get one of these items depending on the part of the body that hurts.

How To Get Involved In Basketball:

There are many leagues around the country that offer to teach basketball. They usually start at a very young age like around 3-4 and most end when the child ends high school. The goal for these leagues are to get the kids better at the game so they can make a high school team, then move up to getting a college scholarship and playing for a college. Also after college if you work hard enough you might be able to be a professional basketball player.

My Recommendations

This is some advice I have when trying to play basketball:

Be ready to work very hard. If you are determined and focused on Basketball you have a chance to make it very far in life.

Practice a lot because if you practice shooting, passing, and dribbling from the start you will get a lot better at the game.

Coaches will be very tough on you to work harder. So be ready to maybe get yelled at if you are doing bad.

Don’t quit because if you quit you will regret it and you won’t play in games as much.

Get good grades in school because most coaches won't even let you on the team if you arent applying yourself with your school work.

How To Get Involved At Home

Watch Basketball games on TV and become a fan. College or NBA are both good.

Get involved with social media. Follow the NBA on Facebook, Twitter, Snapchat and Instagram to see what’s going on in the league.

Workout at home: Everyday do some pushups and situps and pullsups or anything that can help you get better. As the days increase maybe increase the number of workouts you do a day so you can get stronger. Maybe go for a run so you can get faster.

Baseball

If you want to play baseball there are many pieces of equipment you need to have and certain skills you need to have to play the sport and play it at a high level.

How to Play Baseball:

Baseball is a sport more for skill. You need to have very good hand eye coordination to play baseball. Baseball is played by having 2 teams with 9 players. 1 player for each position. One team starts off batting and the other team starts off hitting. The team hitting is going to try to get as many people to touch home plate as possible. There are different ways to get on base. Those ways are hit a single, hit a double, hit a triple, hit a homerun, get a walk, or reach on a error. The team on the field however is going to try and get the batting team out. There are 3 ways for that to happen. The 3 ways are the pitcher strikes the batter out, the batter hits a fly ball and a fielder catches the ball without it touching the ground, or a hitter hitting a ground ball to an infielder and throwing the ball to first base before the hitter gets there. The games are 9 innings and whichever teams that has the most runs by the 9th inning wins the game. If the game is tied the game goes into extra innings until the game is won by 1 team.

Equipment Needed

You will need a bat so you can hit the ball and score some runs.

You will need a glove for two reasons. One so you can catch the ball easier. Two you won't get hurt while catching the ball.

A helmet is very important for baseball because when you are batting sometimes the ball slips out of the Pitchers hand and it could hit the batter in the head and a helmet helps protect that from happening.

Cleats are also very important because if you play baseball in sneakers there is a very good chance that you will slip and hurt yourself, but with cleats they dig into the ground so you don't fall.

Batting gloves are also good to use when you are batting because sometmes if you don't wear batting gloves you can get splinters or you can burn your hands.

How To Get Involved In The Community

There are many ways you can get involved in Baseball in the community. For example see if your local church has a league that you or your child can join. Also tryout for your school team because that can get you scholarships into college and even give you a chance to play professionally. Start when you child is young so they have a lot of time to get better and grow as a player.

My Suggestions

Never quit. In a baseball season you will likely have at least 1 big slump and not do good for a period of time but don’t worry those things happen to the pros too so don't quit.

Practice a lot of hitting and fielding because coaches number 1 thing they want to see is improvement in their players because if they see that they will play you more in the games and in the critical situations. One way you can do this is by asking a friend if they want to go to the park and practice hitting or fielding with you. This might also get your friend into baseball as well.

Coaches are going to be very tough on you but don't take what they say personally they are just trying to motivate you to work harder and do better. If you take what they say personally you will not end up well in your season.

Follow MLB on Twitter, Facebook, Instagram, and Snapchat because sometimes they give tips on how to do stuff.

They also give this information on MLB Network and ESPN.
